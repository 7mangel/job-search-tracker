## Application Tips for Diversity Programs

1. **Highlight First-Gen Status** (if applicable)
   - Mention in essays/interviews
   - Shows resilience & unique perspective

2. **Connect to Heritage** (if comfortable)
   - Hispanic/Latino background = diverse perspective
   - Bilingual = asset

3. **Emphasize Early Interest**
   - Applying as sophomore = proactive
   - Shows commitment to career development

4. **Use Employee Referrals**
   - Connect with Hispanic/Latino employees on LinkedIn
   - Ask about diversity programs
   - Many companies offer referral bonuses

5. **Attend Diversity Conferences**
   - NSHMBA Leadership Institute
   - SHPE National Convention
   - ALPFA Convention
   - Company-specific diversity events
