# Networking Templates

## LinkedIn Connection Request

### Template 1: Alumni Connection
```
Hi [Name],

I'm a sophomore at [School] studying Finance and Computer Science. I noticed you work at [Company] and I'm very interested in their [specific program/role]. 

I'd love to connect and learn more about your experience there, especially as someone looking to break into the industry.

Best,
Miguel
```

### Template 2: Diversity Program Interest
```
Hi [Name],

I'm a first-generation college student interested in [Company]'s diversity programs for sophomores. I saw you're involved with [ERG name] and would love to hear about your experience.

Would you be open to a brief chat about the internship application process?

Thank you,
Miguel Gutierrez
```

### Template 3: Post-Application Follow-up
```
Hi [Name],

I recently applied for the [Role] internship at [Company] and I'm very excited about the opportunity. I noticed your background in [relevant area] and would love to learn more about the team.

Would you be available for a quick informational interview?

Best,
Miguel
```

## Cold Email Template

### Subject: Quick Question About [Company] Internship

```
Hi [Name],

I'm Miguel Gutierrez, a sophomore at [School] studying Finance and Computer Science. I'm very interested in [Company]'s internship program, particularly the [specific program].

As a first-generation college student, I'm passionate about [relevant interest]. I'd love to hear about your experience at [Company] and any advice you might have for the application process.

Would you be available for a 15-minute call next week?

Thank you for your time,
Miguel
[LinkedIn URL]
```

## Follow-up After Interview

```
Hi [Name],

Thank you so much for taking the time to speak with me today. I really enjoyed learning about [specific detail] and I'm even more excited about the opportunity to join [Company].

[Specific detail from conversation that shows you were listening]

I look forward to hearing about next steps. Please let me know if there's anything else I can provide.

Best regards,
Miguel Gutierrez
[Phone] | [Email] | [LinkedIn]
```
